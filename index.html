FAQ

Do you “hack” my devices?

Only with explicit authorization and only in the Full Residential Penetration Test. For families, the network-only option protects your home without accessing devices or personal content.

We have kids. What do you do differently?

We use the Family Network Security Assessment (Network-Only). We secure the router, Wi-Fi, segmentation, DNS protections, and external exposure—without accessing any minors’ devices, accounts, or content.

What about shared devices a child uses sometimes?

Any device used by a child at any time is treated as a child-adjacent device: we do not access it. We protect it with network controls and segmentation instead.

Will this slow down my Wi-Fi?

In most cases, good segmentation and safer DNS won’t noticeably reduce speed. If changes might impact performance, we explain tradeoffs before implementing them.

Do you provide remediation help?

Yes. We can implement router/Wi-Fi hardening, segmentation, DNS protections, and retest. Endpoint/device work is out of scope for the family-safe tier.
